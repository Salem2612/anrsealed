Help

How to use anrsealed.com ?

  1. Select the Expansions.
  2. Select the Number of players.
  3. Select the Number of cards per player.
  4. Select if each player plays with its own cards, or if one player shares its cards to all others.
  5. Select if if you want to use the cards selected by the community of anrsealed, or all the cards of your selected expansions (even the worst !).
  6. Click on Generate & Download.
  7. The Zip file Sealed Packs.zip should have been downloaded.
  8. Read the Sealed Rules bellow to play.

Zip file

The downloaded Zip file contains the following folders:

  • All: All files of the other folders. Usefull for a tournament organisator. Zip each player folder and send them to the corresponding players.
  • Sorted by Alphabetical Order: Useful to import to NetrunnerDB or Jinteki.net with a simple Copy/Paste.
  • Sorted by Card Type: Useful to read your Sealed Pack.

Sealed Rules

Formats

  • One Shot
    • The One Shot is the classic format for one game, or one day tournament.
    • Each player plays a One Shot with 80 cards.
  • Omni-league
    • The Omni-league is a tournament that lasts many weeks or months.
    • Each player starts the Omni-league with 80 cards.
    • The Winner of a game can take a card from the unused cards of its opponent's Sealed pack.
    • Each player wins 5 cards between each round and can modify its decks.

Deckbuilding rules

  • Each player receives:
    • 1 Corp Sealed Pack: Sealed Pack - Player x - Corp.txt
    • 1 Runner Sealed Pack: Sealed Pack - Player x - Runner.txt
    • Optionnaly: The players can Copy/Paste their Sealed Packs to NetrunnerDB or Jinteki.net for better readability.
  • Each player builds 1 Corp deck and 1 Runner deck only with the cards in its Sealed Packs.
  • Each player has an Identity card with no Faction, 45 Cards minimum (or 30 if you chose the 30 cards per deck format), no Influence limit and a blank text box for its Corp and Runner decks. (Mix any cards, even Agendas for the Corp deck)
  • Agenda points Same as in the base game
    • 30-34 cards: 14-15 Agenda points
    • 35-39 cards: 16-17 Agenda points
    • 40-44 cards: 18-19 Agenda points
    • 45-49 cards: 20-21 Agenda points
    • 50-54 cards: 22-23 Agenda points
    • 55-59 cards: 24-25 Agenda points
    • etc...

Win conditions

  • 7 Agenda Points (or 6 Agenda Points if you chose the 30 cards per deck format)
  • The Runner is flatlined
  • The Corp can't draw at the start of its turn

Identity cards

Use these Identity cards for your Sealed games.

Algorithm

All cards of a Sealed Pack are picked randomly by completing the constraints given below. These constraints are given for the 80 cards format. For the 60 cards format, the constraints are 75% of the 80 cards format.

Runner

  • At least 10 EVENT
  • At least 5 HARDWARE
  • At least 2 CONSOLE
  • At least 5 RESOURCE
  • Between 10 and 15 PROGRAM
  • At least 3 FRACTER
  • At least 3 DECODER
  • At least 3 KILLER
  • At least 4 ECO
  • At least 6 ECO_DERIVED

Corp

  • Between 12 and 18 AGENDA
  • At least 7 ASSET
  • At least 5 UPGRADE
  • At least 10 OPERATION
  • Between 20 and 30 ICE
  • At least 5 BARRIER
  • At least 5 CODE_GATE
  • At least 5 SENTRY
  • At least 4 ECO
  • At least 6 ECO_DERIVED
  • At least 2 AMBUSH

List of Cards

The List of Cards is available in a Google Sheet.
You can write comments in the cells to give your opinion on the rarities.

Custom Types

  • ECO : Cards that have a pure gain of credits (Sure Gamble, Private Contracts...).
  • ECO_DERIVED : Cards that have a derived gain of credits (Recurring credits, Lowering install cost, Link...)
