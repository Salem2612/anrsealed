Help

How to use anrsealed.com ?

  1. Select the Extensions.
  2. Select the Number of players.
  3. Select the Number of cards per player.
  4. Select if each player plays with its own cards, or if one player shares its cards to all others.
  5. Click on Generate & Download.
  6. The Zip file Sealed Packs.zip should have been downloaded.
  7. Read the Sealed Rules bellow to play.

Zip file

The downloaded Zip file contains the following folders:

  • All: All files of the other folders. Usefull for a tournament organisator. Send the files to the corresponding players.
  • Sorted by Alphabetical Order: Useful to import to NetrunnerDB or Jinteki.net.
  • Sorted by Rarity: Useful to see your best cards.

Sealed Rules

Formats

  • One Shot
    • The One Shot is the classic format for one game, or one day tournament.
    • Each player plays a One Shot with 60 cards.
  • Omni-league
    • The Omni-league is a tournament that lasts many weeks or months.
    • Each player starts the Omni-league with 60 cards.
    • The Winner of a game can take a card from the unused cards of its opponent's Sealed pack.
    • Each player wins 5 cards between each round and can modify its decks.

Deckbuilding rules

  • Each player receives:
    • 1 Corp Sealed Pack: Sealed Pack - Player x - Corp.txt
    • 1 Runner Sealed Pack: Sealed Pack - Player x - Runner.txt
    • Optionnaly: The players can Copy/Paste their Sealed Packs to NetrunnerDB or Jinteki.net for better readability.
  • Each player builds 1 Corp deck and 1 Runner deck only with the cards in its Sealed Packs.
  • Each player has an Identity card with no Faction, 30 Cards minimum, no Influence limit and a blank text box for its Corp and Runner decks. (Mix any cards, even Agendas for the Corp deck)
  • Agenda points
    • 30-34 cards: 14-15 Agenda points
    • 35-39 cards: 16-17 Agenda points
    • 40-44 cards: 18-19 Agenda points
    • 45-49 cards: 20-21 Agenda points
    • 50-54 cards: 22-23 Agenda points
    • etc...

Win conditions Same as in the draft game

  • 6 Agenda Points
  • The Runner is flatlined
  • The Corp can't draw at the start of its turn

Identity cards

Use these Identity cards for your Sealed games.

Starter

  • Each Starter contains 60 Corp cards and 60 Runner Cards. (46 COMMON, 12 UNCO and 2 RARE per side)
  • Few cards of the Starter are picked randomly by completing some constraints given below:
Runner
  • Exactly 1 RARE for 19 COMON
  • Among the COMMON cards :
    • At least 3 FRACTER
    • At least 3 DECODER
    • At least 3 KILLER
    • At least 1 TUTOR
    • At least 3 ECO
    • At least 5 ECO_DERIVED
Corp
  • Exactly 1 RARE for 19 COMON
  • Among the COMMON cards :
    • At least 12 AGENDA
    • At least 12 ICE
    • At least 4 BARRIER
    • At least 4 CODE_GATE
    • At least 4 SENTRY
    • At least 3 ECO
    • At least 5 ECO_DERIVED
    • At least 2 AMBUSH

List of Cards

The List of Cards is available in a Google Sheet.
You can write comments in the cells to give your opinion on the rarities.

Rarities

  • BANNED : Cards that have been judged too strong (2/1 Agendas), or too useless in Sealed format (Hivemind...).
  • RARE : Powerful cards that are limited to only one copy.
  • UNCO : Unused rarity.
  • COMMON : Ordinary cards that are limited to 3 copies.

Custom Types

  • ECO : Cards that have a pure gain of credits (Sure Gamble, Private Contracts...).
  • ECO_DERIVED : Cards that have a derived gain of credits (Recurring credits, Lowering install cost, Link...)
