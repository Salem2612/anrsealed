Help

How to use anrsealed.com ?

  1. Choose the Number of players.
  2. Select the Number of Starters and Boosters for each player.
  3. Click on Generate & Download.
  4. The Zip file Sealed Packs.zip should have been downloaded.
  5. Read the Sealed Rules bellow to play.

Zip file

The downloaded Zip file contains the following folders:

  • All: All files of the other folders. Usefull for a tournament organisator. Send the files to the corresponding players.
  • Sorted by Alphabetical Order: Useful to import to NetrunnerDB or Jinteki.net.
  • Sorted by Rarity: Useful to see your best cards.
  • Sorted by Starter and Boosters: Useful to see which cards come from which Starter and Boosters.

Sealed Rules

Formats

  • One Shot
    • The One Shot is the classic format for one game or one day tournament.
    • Each player plays a One Shot with 1 Starter and 4 Boosters.
  • Omni-league
    • The Omni-league is a tournament that lasts many weeks or months.
    • Each player starts the Omni-league with 1 Starter and 3 Boosters.
    • The Winner of a game can take a card from the unused cards of its opponent's Sealed pack.
    • Each player wins 1 Booster between each round and can modify its decks.

Deckbuilding rules

  • Each player receives:
    • 1 Corp Sealed Pack: Sealed Pack - Player x - Corp.txt
    • 1 Runner Sealed Pack: Sealed Pack - Player x - Runner.txt
    • Optionnaly: The players can Copy/Paste their Sealed Packs to NetrunnerDB or Jinteki.net for better readability.
  • Each player builds 1 Corp deck and 1 Runner deck only with the cards in its Sealed Packs.
  • Each player has an Identity card with no Faction, 45 Cards minimum, no Influence limit and a blank text box for its Corp and Runner decks. (Mix any cards, even Agendas for the Corp deck)
  • Agenda points
    • 45-49 cards: 18-19 Agenda points
    • 50-54 cards: 20-21 Agenda points
    • etc...

Win conditions Same as in the base game

  • 7 Agenda Points
  • The Runner is flatlined
  • The Corp can't draw at the start of its turn

Identity cards

Use these Identity cards for your Sealed games.

Starter

  • Each Starter contains 60 Corp cards and 60 Runner Cards. (46 COMMON, 12 UNCO and 2 RARE per side)
  • Each card can be picked only 1 time in the Base Set Starter.
  • Few cards of the Starter are picked randomly by completing some constraints given below:
Runner
  • Exactly 2 RARE
  • Exactly 12 UNCO
  • Exactly 46 COMMON
  • Among the COMMON cards :
    • At least 3 FRACTER
    • At least 3 DECODER
    • At least 3 KILLER
    • At least 1 TUTOR
    • At least 4 ECO
    • At least 8 ECO_DERIVED
Corp
  • Exactly 2 RARE
  • Exactly 12 UNCO
  • Exactly 46 COMMON
  • Among the COMMON cards :
    • At least 12 AGENDA
    • At least 15 ICE
    • At least 5 BARRIER
    • At least 5 CODE_GATE
    • At least 5 SENTRY
    • At least 4 ECO
    • At least 6 ECO_DERIVED
    • At least 2 AMBUSH

Booster

  • Each Booster contains 7 Corp cards and 7 Runner Cards. (4 COMMON, 2 UNCO and 1 RARE per side)
  • Each card can be picked 1 or 2 times in the Boosters.

Base Set

  • The Base Set Starter and Base Set Boosters contain cards from the Core Set, all Deluxes and the Genesis Cycle.

List of Cards

The List of Cards is available in a Google Sheet.
You can write comments in the cells to give your opinion on the rarities.

Rarities

  • BANNED : Cards that have been judged too strong (2/1 Agendas), or too useless in Sealed format (Hivemind...).
  • RARE : Powerful cards. You should play your RARE cards.
  • UNCO : Good cards. You should play many of your UNCO cards.
  • COMMON : Ordinary cards.

Custom Types

  • ECO : Cards that have a pure gain of credits (Sure Gamble, Private Contracts...).
  • ECO_DERIVED : Cards that have a derived gain of credits (Recurring credits, Lowering install cost, Link...)
